about the site

nerdcore was created in 2001 as an outlet for random ramblings and has served that purpose for the past 10 years. The content featured here is diverse, but mostly in the realm of technology/nerdy/music related topics.

The author is Jesse Hixson, a web programmer from western Pennsylvania. He likes cats, heavy metal and TV cooking shows.

This blog was originally powered by static HTML files, then driven by blogging engines, usually WordPress. It is the author's opionion that WordPress has become a bit convoluted recent versions and is no longer needed.

For fun and extra nerd-cred we're back to static HTML, this time served up by Jekyll on GitHub.
